Profile

Home is Toronto with my wife and daughter. Family life is at the center of how I spend my time and what matters most to me.

I work in design and development, and I also make electronic music. Across all of it, I enjoy understanding how things work, finding practical ways to improve them, and building experiences that are useful in everyday life.

I'm a Third-Culture Kid who spent his teenage years abroad. Growing up in different places gave me a broad perspective and a strong habit of adapting, learning quickly, and solving problems from more than one angle.
