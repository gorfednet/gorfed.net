Design

For over 20 years, I have led UX strategy and delivery across complex digital products and services, helping teams turn ambiguity into clear, user-centred outcomes.

My work spans discovery through execution, with a systems mindset shaped by hands-on development experience and long-standing collaboration across design, product, and engineering.

I focus on practical innovation at scale, balancing user value, business goals, and technical feasibility to ship experiences that perform in the real world.

I'm currently employed by TD Bank as an Experience Design Manager, responsible for leading Direct Investing and Wealth Management initiatives. To date, TD has patented two of my inventions.
