Apps

I build products beyond my day-to-day leadership work to explore opportunities at the intersection of UX, technology, and business value.

These efforts span end-to-end product development: opportunity framing, experience design, front-end and back-end implementation, and iterative release strategy informed by real usage.

Some are practical utilities, some are exploratory concepts, and some are entrepreneurial bets. Together, they sharpen my ability to move from insight to shipped outcomes with speed and accountability.

Interests

  • Workflow, productivity, and operational tooling

  • Creative and audio-adjacent software

  • Automation, scripting, and developer enablement

  • AI-assisted product and interface experimentation

  • Data-informed MVPs and rapid concept validation

  • Cross-platform utilities with clear everyday value

  • Game mechanics and interaction prototyping

Platforms

  • Web applications and responsive product surfaces

  • Native desktop and mobile (macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, Android); hybrid or cross-platform when a shared stack better fits roadmap, parity, or maintenance

  • Cross-platform CLI and local developer tools

  • Browser extensions and lightweight in-browser utilities

  • API-backed services and integration workflows

  • Prototyping and proof-of-concept environments
